Ouranós
**=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=***=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=**

The clouds pass high up
Drifting alone

The sun falls
Filling it with a deep dark dusk violet
Encased with brilliant blues, blooming

The sun now in a deep slumber
The dead stars in the sky
Painting along it
The soft humming of the light
Away from the luna

Soft, cold wind howls
Silently, filling the emptiness

Looking down
Smiling, crying
At death and life
Around it all

Drifting alone
Wondering

It darkens
Crying yet smiling

Its alone
Away from down here
Up there, it is alone
But down here it is not alone
It is with us

Limited
Yet it is boundless
Boundless for those who believe in it 

Epitélous o ouranós
